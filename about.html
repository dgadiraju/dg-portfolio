About Me

Passionate about transforming data into actionable insights and building innovative AI solutions

I'm a seasoned technology leader with over 15 years of experience in AI, data analytics, and product development. Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of working with organizations ranging from innovative startups to Fortune 100 companies, helping them harness the power of data to drive meaningful business outcomes.

My passion lies at the intersection of technology and education. As the founder of ITVersity, I've dedicated myself to democratizing access to data science and analytics education, having taught over 100,000 students worldwide. I believe that knowledge sharing and mentorship are fundamental to advancing our industry and creating opportunities for the next generation of data professionals.

Fun fact: I've hosted over 50 podcast episodes, interviewing industry experts and sharing insights about the evolving landscape of AI and data science.
